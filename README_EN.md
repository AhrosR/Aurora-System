### AI-Assisted Conflict Resolution
(Premium Module + )

Aurora actively monitors community interactions to maintain a healthy environment.

When conflict, harassment, spam or toxic behawior is detected:

* Mia may **enter the conversation** and attempt to de-escalate the situation using warm, calming language
* A **detailed private report** is automatically sent to the responsible moderator or administrator, including:

- channel location
- involved users
- full conversational context

If no moderator responds within a defined time:

* Mia performs an **AI-based analysis** of the situation
* Applies **temporary and reversible restrictions** (e.g. limited messaging) to involved users
* No permanent actions (bans/kicks) are executed automatically

Once a human moderator joins:

* Mia **withdraws gracefully**, handing control back to the administrator

This system is designed to **support human moderation, not replace it**.
